## Project Overview

**Eternal Bastion** is a tactical defense game set in a dark medieval fantasy world. Unlike traditional tower defense games, players must combine the strategic placement of **Defensive Structures** with the deployment of **Guardians (Characters)** who physically intercept and fight the "Void" hordes on the battlefield.

### Key Features
* **Hybrid Defense:** Strategic mix of long-range firepower and melee frontline combat.
* **Physical Blocking:** Characters have collision and can halt enemy movement to engage in combat.
* **Tactical Choke Points:** Success depends on creating areas where enemies are stalled under tower fire.
* **Dark Fantasy Atmosphere:** A gritty aesthetic focusing on a kingdom's last stand against ancient magic.

### The Arsenal
The defense is based on managing two main pillars to protect the realm:

| Type | Unit/Building | Special Role | Physical Attribute |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Character** | Royal Paladin | **Frontline Tank** | High health and blocking capacity |
| **Character** | Shadow Rogue | **Stealth DPS** | Fast attacks and critical damage |
| **Structure** | Ballista Tower | **Long Range** | High piercing damage to single targets |
| **Structure** | Runed Altar | **Arcane AoE** | Magic pulses that damage groups |
